Christmas Tree Cheese Ball

Christmas Tree Cheese Ball

This Christmas tree cheese ball is quickly shaped, and easily decorated. Pineapple flavored cream cheese spread forms the tree, with rosemary sprigs for branches. Raspberries, pistachios, and star sprinkles make the red and green ornaments, with cheese cube presents arrayed below.

Prep Time Total Time Servings
20 mins 20 mins 12

Ingredients

  • plastic wrap
  • cooking spray
  • 2 (7.5 ounce) packages pineapple flavored cream cheese spread, such as Philadelphia® brand
  • 1/2 pint fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup shelled pistachios, or as needed
  • several small sprigs fresh rosemary
  • star-shaped baking sprinkles
  • Colby and Monterrey Jack mixed cheese cubes, to taste
  • 6 red wax-wrapped mini cheeses, such as BabyBel®
  • assorted crackers, for serving

Directions

  1. Spray one side of a 12-inch square piece of plastic wrap with cooking spray and place plastic, sprayed side up, on a work surface.
  2. Remove cream cheese spread from containers, and stack on the sprayed plastic. Lift plastic and place on spread, and mold into a cone shape using your hands.
  3. Carefully slide cream cheese cone onto a decorative plate.
  4. Dot cone with fresh raspberries, with the holes facing out. Fill each hole with a shelled pistachio, and dot the cone with pistachios. Make a row of raspberries around the bottom of the cone, gently pushing in, toward the center of the cone.
  5. Insert several fresh rosemary sprigs around the cone to imitate tree branches. Decorate with star-shaped baking sprinkle “ornaments,” randomly placed.
  6. Use cheese cubes around base of the cone to simulate packages, and add a few red wax-wrapped mini cheeses. Serve immediately, with crackers.
